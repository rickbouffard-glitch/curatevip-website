Your privacy is fundamental to how CURATEvip is designed. We collect only what we need to deliver a precise, personalized experience — and we do not sell your personal information to third parties. Ever.

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to RMK Group LLC, a Delaware limited liability company, doing business as CURATEvip. We operate the applications and websites accessible at curatevip.app and curatevip.ai (collectively, the "Service"). References to "we," "us," or "our" in this Policy refer to RMK Group LLC.

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: privacy@curatevip.app

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Information you provide directly:

  • Name and email address when requesting early access or creating an account
  • Speed Quiz responses — your preferences across Stay, Table, and Social environments
  • Location or destination preferences you enter in the app
  • Messages you send us via contact or support forms

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage data such as features accessed, screens viewed, and session duration
  • IP address and approximate geographic region
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics

Information from third parties:

  • If you sign in using a third-party service (e.g., Apple or Google), we receive basic profile information such as name and email as permitted by that service

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Build and continuously refine your Speed Profile to deliver accurate, personalized venue curation
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send transactional communications such as account confirmations and service updates
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not use your Speed Quiz responses or taste profile data for advertising targeting, and we do not sell or license this data to third parties.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted vendors who help us operate the Service (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics, email delivery), bound by confidentiality obligations and prohibited from using your data for their own purposes
  • Legal compliance: When required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of CURATEvip, its users, or the public
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case we will notify you and your data will remain subject to this Policy
  • With your consent: For any other purpose with your explicit prior consent

5. Taste Profile & AI-Generated Data

Your Speed Profile — the taste fingerprint derived from your quiz responses and in-app behavior — is the core of the CURATEvip experience. This profile is used exclusively to power your personal curation results. It is not shared with venues, advertisers, or any third party without your explicit consent.

We may use aggregated, de-identified data (which cannot reasonably be used to identify you) to improve our curation algorithms and service quality.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you request deletion of your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain certain information or where data is needed to resolve disputes or enforce our agreements.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or alteration. These include encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews.

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Port your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt out of certain data processing activities
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@curatevip.app. We will respond within 30 days. We do not discriminate against users who exercise their privacy rights.

9. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To submit a request, contact privacy@curatevip.app.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete it promptly. If you believe we have such information, contact us at privacy@curatevip.app.

We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals, as no industry-wide standard has been established for such signals.
